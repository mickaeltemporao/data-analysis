## Structure
- **1 page** = ~5 paragraphs
- **1 paragraph** =  ~5 sentences = 1 idea
    - The first sentence in a paragraph introduces the idea.
    - Followed by three sentences written has arguments supporting the idea.
    - The last sentence concludes the idea.
- **1 sentence ** = ~20 words
